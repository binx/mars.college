<div id="container">
    <p>
        Academics on Mars revolve around the core majors. Everyone at Mars College has to declare at least one major, attend all its regular sessions, and make a project for midterm and final. Mars Residents are also encouraged to declare majors.
    </p>
    <h2>
        <span id="clubs">
            Majors
        </span>
    </h2>
    <p>
        <b>AI</b>: Making AI agents who create images, sounds, and texts, i.e. prompt engineering and finetuning large language and text-to-image models. Some of us are building <a href="https://eden.art">Eden</a>, which we use <a href="https://mars.college/2022/07/12/Eden.html">to build live/interactive web applications and bots</a> serving these models.
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Off-grid shelter</b>: urban design with pallet racks, mobile domiciles, and building components, making Mars sustainable and habitable.
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Art and craft</b>: Fine arts on Mars and Bombay Beach, street art, sculpture, and ceramics. With our friends at BBAC, we will paint murals and setup workshops with kilns and welding equipment.
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>3d design</b>: Using software to create 3d worlds, towards a virtual counterpart of Mars for games, metaverses, and multiverses. 
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>DAOs and crypto</b>: Practice emerging protocols for decentralization and coordination, thinking about how to apply it to the Martian enterprise. 
    </p>
    <h2>
        <span id="clubs">
            Clubs
        </span>
    </h2>
    <p>
        Besides for the core majors, a number of more informal clubs are active on Mars.
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Yoga</b>: Regular yoga, meditation, and breathing exercises. 
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Electric unicycling</b>: we are avid fans of the electric unicycle, and go on <a href="/gallery/electric_unicycle">group rides around the surrounding area</a>. <a href="/euc">We have some unicycles available</a>. 
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Bouldering</b>: we build a <a href="/gallery/bouldering">bouldering wall</a> onto the pallet racks on Mars, and practice for a trip to Joshua Tree.
    </p>
    <p>
        <b>Live coding</b>: There is <a href="/gallery/performance">a live-code scene on Mars</a>. We'll organize some algoraves and live streams.
    </p>
    <h2>
        <span id="clubs">
            Midterm and final
        </span>
    </h2>
    <p>
        Weekend of Feb 17 we'll have our midterm, during which we'll organize show & tell, and majors should formulate an idea for a finals project by then. 
    </p>
    <p>
        Our final will be the weekend of March 31, and/or coinciding with the <a href="http://www.bombaybeachbiennale.org/">biennale</a>. For final, majors present final projects and we organize a group show around it.
    </p>
